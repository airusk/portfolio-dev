Ever since getting my hands on that unplugged PlayStation controller (younger siblings might relate to this experience), I have been obsessed with the ways we can interact as our virtual selves. It's no surprise that I pursued a degree in Computer Science, and soon learned to craft web applications from the ground up. While the entire architecture is a joy to build, I find my self marvelling at well-thought-out interactive systems. From the tiniest details to the grand features, everything creates an understanding between the user and the product.
